31K+ Pressure Washing Leads

Web Scraping & Lead Generation Case Study

Overview

Built a large-scale lead generation dataset by scraping pressure washing business listings across multiple regions. The dataset includes verified contact information, ratings, and location data for targeted outreach.

Problem

Businesses needed a reliable lead database for outreach campaigns. Manual research was slow, inconsistent, and lacked structured data for targeting.

Approach

  • Scraped multiple business directories
  • Extracted contact details and locations
  • Removed duplicates and invalid records
  • Structured clean dataset for outreach
