31K+ Pressure Washing Leads

Web Scraping & Lead Generation Case Study

Overview

Scraped and structured 31K+ pressure washing businesses including contact details, ratings, and locations. Built a clean dataset for outreach campaigns and market analysis.

Problem

Manual lead generation was slow and inconsistent. Businesses required a structured dataset to target prospects efficiently and reduce research time.

Approach

  • Scraped multiple business directories
  • Extracted contact details and ratings
  • Removed duplicates and invalid entries
  • Structured clean dataset for outreach
  • Validated contact information
  • Prepared dataset for marketing campaigns
