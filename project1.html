31K+ Pressure Washing Leads

Web Scraping & Lead Generation Project

Problem

Businesses often struggle to find structured and reliable local service provider data. Information is scattered across multiple platforms, making manual data collection slow, inconsistent, and inefficient. This creates a major bottleneck for running targeted outreach and marketing campaigns.

Approach

Built a scalable Python-based scraping system to extract business listings from multiple sources. Implemented data cleaning pipelines to remove duplicates, standardize formats, and enrich records with contact information such as emails, phone numbers, and locations. The final dataset was structured for direct use in lead generation workflows.

Impact

Generated over 31,000 verified business records, significantly reducing manual research effort. Enabled highly targeted outreach campaigns, improving efficiency and increasing the potential for higher conversion rates.
