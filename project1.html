31K+ Pressure Washing Leads

Built a large-scale web scraping pipeline to generate verified business leads for outreach and market analysis.

Problem

Businesses lacked access to structured and verified local service provider data, making targeted outreach inefficient and time-consuming.

Approach

Developed a scraping pipeline to extract business listings from multiple sources, clean the data, and structure it into a usable dataset for lead generation.

Key Results

  • 31,000+ verified business records collected
  • Extracted emails, phone numbers, and locations
  • Structured dataset ready for outreach campaigns
