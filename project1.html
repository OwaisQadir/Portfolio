31K+ Pressure Washing Leads

Web Scraping & Lead Generation Project

Overview

Built a large-scale lead generation dataset by scraping pressure washing businesses across multiple locations. The dataset includes contact details, ratings, and location information for targeted outreach campaigns.

Problem

Manual lead generation was time-consuming and inconsistent. Businesses needed structured data for outreach campaigns and market analysis.

Approach

  • Scraped business listings from multiple directories
  • Extracted phone, email, ratings and locations
  • Cleaned duplicates and invalid entries
  • Structured dataset for outreach

Tools & Technologies

Python BeautifulSoup Scrapy Pandas APIs Excel

Key Deliverables

31K Lead Dataset Clean CSV Export Contact Database Location Segmentation Duplicate Removal

Results & Impact

  • 31,000+ leads generated
  • Reduced manual research time by 90%
  • Improved outreach targeting
  • Ready-to-use dataset for campaigns
