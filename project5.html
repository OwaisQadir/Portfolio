British Airways Data Science Job Simulation

Customer Insights & Predictive Modeling

Overview

Completed a data science simulation focused on how analytics supports British Airways' business decisions. The project involved scraping customer review data, performing exploratory data analysis, and building a predictive model to understand factors influencing customer buying behaviour.

Problem

Airlines receive large volumes of customer feedback, making it difficult to manually identify patterns influencing purchasing decisions. Without structured analysis, valuable insights remain hidden and cannot be leveraged to improve customer experience and marketing strategies.

Approach

  • Scraped airline customer review data
  • Cleaned and structured dataset
  • Performed exploratory data analysis
  • Identified sentiment patterns
  • Built predictive machine learning model
  • Visualized key insights

  • Built predictive analytics capability
  • Extracted insights from large review dataset
