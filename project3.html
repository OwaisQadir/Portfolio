E-Commerce Performance Dashboard

Interactive Sales & KPI Analytics

Overview

Developed an interactive Ecommerce Performance Dashboard to track sales performance, profitability, and product insights. The dashboard provides visibility into revenue, profit margin, order volume, and growth trends, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven business decisions.

Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data but often lack centralized reporting. Without a clear dashboard, it becomes difficult to track performance, identify top products, and understand sales trends.

Approach

  • Cleaned ecommerce sales dataset
  • Created KPI metrics for performance tracking
  • Built interactive dashboard
  • Analyzed revenue and profit trends
  • Identified top-performing products
  • Visualized category performance
