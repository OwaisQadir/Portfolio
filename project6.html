Business Data Visualisation

Executive Dashboard & Business Insights

Overview

Completed a data visualization project focused on transforming business data into clear executive-level insights. The dashboard highlights revenue trends, regional performance, customer behaviour, and operational metrics to support leadership decision-making.

Problem

Business stakeholders often struggle to interpret large datasets and identify actionable insights. Without clear visualization, decision-making becomes slower and less effective. A structured dashboard was required to simplify complex data.

Approach

  • Analyzed business dataset
  • Defined key executive KPIs
  • Created dashboard layout
  • Visualized revenue trends
  • Built regional performance insights
  • Designed executive-level visuals
