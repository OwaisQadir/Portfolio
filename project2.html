Real Estate Web/Data Scraping

Property Investment Analysis Case Study

Overview

Scraped and analyzed property listings from Kijiji.ca and Rentals.ca using Python to identify profitable real estate investment opportunities. The dataset included property price, location, number of bedrooms, rental type, and additional listing information. This enabled data-driven investment decisions and improved market analysis.

Problem

Real estate investors often rely on manual browsing across multiple platforms to compare property prices and identify profitable areas. This process is slow, inconsistent, and lacks structured insights. Without consolidated data, identifying high-return investment opportunities becomes difficult and time-consuming.

Approach

  • Scraped property listings from Kijiji and Rentals.ca
  • Extracted price, location, bedrooms, and listing details
  • Cleaned and structured dataset for analysis
  • Analyzed pricing trends across cities
  • Identified high ROI investment areas
  • Visualized insights for decision-making
