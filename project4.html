Production Analytics Dashboard

Manufacturing KPI & Efficiency Tracking

Overview

Developed a production analytics dashboard to monitor manufacturing performance, track operational efficiency, and identify bottlenecks. The dashboard provides visibility into production output, downtime, quality metrics, and workforce productivity to support operational decision-making.

Problem

Manufacturing teams relied on manual reports and spreadsheets to monitor production. This made it difficult to track efficiency, identify downtime drivers, and measure performance. A centralized analytics dashboard was required to provide real-time visibility into operations.

Approach

  • Prepared and cleaned production dataset
  • Defined manufacturing KPIs
  • Built interactive analytics dashboard
  • Tracked production efficiency metrics
  • Analyzed downtime drivers
  • Visualized workforce performance
